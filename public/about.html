About Deshakthi Lanka

Bridging Sri Lankan talent with global opportunities since 2014

Our Story

Deshakthi Lanka was established with a vision to bridge the gap between talented Sri Lankan professionals and the global job market. With years of industry expertise, we have grown into a leading recruitment agency, dedicated to transforming lives by providing reliable, efficient, and life-changing international career opportunities for thousands of aspiring candidates.

Our success is built on a foundation of transparency, integrity, and unwavering commitment to our clients. We believe in treating every job seeker with respect and fairness, ensuring a recruitment process that adheres to the highest international standards. Excellence in service and empowering our workforce remain the core principles that drive everything we do.

Today, Deshakthi Lanka stands as a beacon of hope for Sri Lankan professionals seeking to expand their horizons. We've successfully placed over 5,000 candidates in prestigious organizations across the globe, maintaining a remarkable 95% satisfaction rate from both employers and employees alike.
