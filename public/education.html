Global Industry Requirements

Different countries and industries have strict educational and vocational standards. Here is a quick guide to the minimum qualifications expected by our international partners.

Culinary & Hospitality

Primary Markets: Dubai, Maldives, Europe

  • Executive Chefs / Sous Chefs: Minimum NVQ Level 4 in Commercial Cookery or Culinary Arts Diploma.
  • Food Safety: Basic Food Hygiene Certificate (HACCP preferred).
  • Front Office / F&B: Diploma in Hospitality Management & fluent English proficiency.

Healthcare & Nursing

Primary Markets: UK, Saudi Arabia, Japan

  • Registered Nurses (UK): BSc in Nursing, active SLMC registration, and NMC CBT/OSCE clearance.
  • Language (UK/Japan): IELTS Academic (7.0) or JLPT (N3/N4) for Japan.

Construction & Engineering

Primary Markets: Qatar, UAE, Singapore

  • Civil/MEP Engineers: BSc/BEng in related field (Washington Accord recognized preferred).
  • Technicians/Electricians: NVQ Level 3 or 4 in specific trade from recognized institutes.

Specialized Caregiving

Primary Markets: Japan, Israel, Canada

  • General Caregivers: NVQ Level 3 or 4 in Caregiving from TVEC registered institutions.
  • Japan SSW Visa: Careworker Skills Test & JLPT N4 / JFT-Basic A2.
